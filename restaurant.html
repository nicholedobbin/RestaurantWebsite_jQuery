Pizza Perfect

Our Story

We are a family-run pizza restaurant with the best Japanese and Italian inspired pizza in town. After years of living in Japan and Italy, we decided to settle in sunny Cape Town and open our very own restaurant.

Our Food

All of our ingredients are locally and ethically sourced. We make our own pizza dough using methods learned in Italy and we make our own cheese using techniques learned in Japan. Our burrata is the best in town!

Our People

As a family-run business we prioritise community. Our team is passionate, skilled and ready to serve you your favourite pizza.
